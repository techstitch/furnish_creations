Service Policy

At Furnish Creation, we provide doorstep as well as workshop-based sofa repair and restoration services. Our offerings include repair of sofas, chairs, recliners, beds, almirahs, cabinets, dining tables, office chairs, and antique sofa. Every repair is carried out by skilled craftsmen with over 25+ years of experience in wood, leather, and fabric restoration.

Customers are responsible for providing accurate details regarding sofa condition during the booking. We may inspect the item before confirming the final quotation.

Warranty Policy

All repair and restoration services come with a limited service warranty, covering workmanship and material defects. Warranty does not apply to:

  • Damage caused by mishandling, misuse, or accidents after delivery.
  • Normal wear and tear of fabric, leather, foam, or polish.
  • Repairs or alterations done by third-party service providers.

Pricing & Estimates

Furnish Creation follows a transparent and affordable pricing model. Customers can save up to 70% compared to buying new sofa. All estimates are shared in advance and confirmed with customer approval before starting the work. Additional costs (if any) are communicated clearly.
